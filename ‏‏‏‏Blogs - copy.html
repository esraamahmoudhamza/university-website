Delta University partners with Coursera to prepare students for jobs of the future


Becoming the first private university in Egypt to fully integrate Coursera for Campus into its curriculum to create opportunities and improve employability of graduates, this partnership will enable the university’s students to have unlimited enrolments to Coursera’s catalogue and access to world-class courses taught by over 275 leading university and industry partners, including Imperial College London, École Polytechnique, IBM, and Microsoft, among other global partners. Furthermore, students will benefit from a world-class online, mobile, and hands-on learning experience while earning job- relevant credentials for courses they complete. The Coursera catalogue currently includes 5,200+ courses, 3,300+ guided projects, 650+ specialisations, and 115+ certificates.
