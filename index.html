College Levels

We have four levels in the Faculty of Artificial Intelligence

Level One

In the first level of the college, students study some of the important basics required to study the important subjects that qualify them for the labor market.

Level Two

In the second level of the college, students study some important subjects such as networks and other subjects required in the labor market

Level Three

In the third level of the college, students begin to divide into three departments, so that each student chooses the appropriate department for him to study.

Level Four

In the fourth level of the college, students complete the appropriate field for them, which qualifies them for the labor market
